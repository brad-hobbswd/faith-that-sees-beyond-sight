Introduction

Life often places people in moments where the path ahead appears hidden. Questions rise without answers. Prayers seem suspended in silence. Circumstances speak louder than hope. Human eyes naturally search for visible evidence before taking another step.

Faith rarely begins with visible evidence. Faith begins where certainty ends.

These pages follow a journey through unseen seasons, quiet battles, dreams, unexpected encounters, and moments where Jesus reveals that heaven often moves long before human eyes recognize what He has already prepared.

Brad's story carries struggles that many understand deeply. Fear, uncertainty, hidden pain, and the desire to trust Jesus while walking through places where nothing appears to change.

Every chapter reveals a greater truth. The unseen realm is never empty. Jesus still speaks. Jesus still guides. Jesus still prepares pathways before footsteps arrive.

"Faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen."

Hebrews 11:1
