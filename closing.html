Closing

Life rarely unfolds according to human expectations. Certain seasons feel clear while others carry uncertainty and unanswered questions. Many people spend years waiting until every detail becomes visible before taking another step.

Faith often works differently. Faith asks people to trust before understanding arrives. Faith moves before visible proof appears. Faith believes Jesus is already working long before circumstances reveal what heaven prepared.

Brad discovered that unseen places were never empty. Moments of silence carried purpose. Unexpected detours carried direction. Difficult seasons carried growth. Every hidden place carried the presence of Jesus.

Human eyes naturally search for evidence. Faith learns to search for Jesus.

Certain paths remain hidden only until footsteps begin moving. Certain answers remain unseen only until trust takes another step. Purpose often waits on the other side of obedience.

Faith is not blind. Faith sees beyond sight.

This journey may have reached a final page, though faith never reaches a final chapter. New paths still wait. New purpose still unfolds. Jesus still leads. The journey continues.
