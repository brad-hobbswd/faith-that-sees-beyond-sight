Dedication

This book is dedicated to every person who has stood in seasons where answers seemed hidden and hope felt stretched beyond comfort.

For every heart that kept praying while heaven felt quiet. For every soul that continued believing while circumstances looked impossible. For every person who walked through uncertainty while carrying faith one step at a time.

This journey is also dedicated to Gabriel and Justin, whose presence throughout these pages reflects love, encouragement, friendship, and reminders that Jesus often speaks through people standing close beside us.

Most importantly, this book is dedicated to Jesus. The One who walks ahead before pathways become visible. The One who still speaks in quiet places. The One who remains faithful when human eyes cannot yet see what heaven already prepared.

Bradley Hobbs
