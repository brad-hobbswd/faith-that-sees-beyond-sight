CHAPTER FOUR

The Mountain of Quiet Decisions

Life continued moving forward. Brad still faced ordinary struggles, yet something had changed inside him. Greater awareness followed him through daily life. Small choices now carried deeper meaning. Faith was not proven only through dramatic moments. Faith revealed itself through quiet decisions.

Trust appeared through daily surrender. Trust appeared through small acts of obedience. Trust appeared through refusing fear.

Justin met with Brad regularly throughout this season. Conversations stretched beyond ordinary friendship. Faith. Family. Purpose. Prayer. Their friendship strengthened Brad in places where isolation once held influence.

One afternoon Justin shared a simple truth that settled deeply within him.

“You want mountains to move.”

“Mountains move because of small decisions made quietly when nobody else notices.”

Brad reflected on those words long afterward. Large breakthroughs had always captured his attention. Steady obedience rarely had.

Faith suddenly looked different. Large leaps no longer felt necessary. Trust looked more like small footsteps. Steady movement. Daily surrender. Simple obedience.

Brad began journaling moments where faith replaced fear. Small choices filled page after page. Moments where he listened for the whisper of Jesus rather than the noise of doubt. Moments where trust spoke louder than uncertainty.

Slowly change emerged. Someone who once walked with uncertainty gradually stood with confidence. Someone who once questioned purpose slowly discovered direction. Someone who feared unseen places now trusted the One who already stood within them.

Faith was reshaping him from the inside outward.
